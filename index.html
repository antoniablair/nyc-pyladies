NYC PyLadies

We're the NYC chapter of PyLadies, an international community for women and non-binary folks in tech. Come to our free Meetup events to make new friends and learn more about Python programming.

Our events are a mix of lightning talks, workshops, networking, study groups, and more! All skill levels and industries are welcome.

Interested in giving a talk? We'd love to hear from you!

RSVP for our events on our Meetup page. You can also follow us on Twitter.

About PyLadies

PyLadies is an international mentorship group with a focus on helping more women become active participants and leaders in the Python open-source community. Our mission is to promote, educate and advance a diverse Python community through outreach, education, conferences, events and social gatherings.
