NYC PyLadies

We're the NYC chapter of PyLadies, an international community of women and non-binary people in tech.

We host monthly events, talks, and study groups where you can meet other Python programmers and learn more about programming, data science, web development, and more.

Events

Join our Meetup group to hear about our free events in the NYC metro area. All skill levels are welcome!

Interested in hosting us, giving a talk, or leading a workshop? We'd love to hear from you!

About Us

PyLadies is an international mentorship group with a focus on helping more women become active participants and leaders in the Python open-source community.

Our mission is to promote, educate and advance a diverse Python community through outreach, education, conferences, events and social gatherings.

To learn more, visit the PyLadies website.

Connect

Follow us on Twitter or join our Meetup group to get our announcements and find out about upcoming events.

If you would like to give a tech talk, demo, or workshop, contact us at nyc@pyladies.com.

Interested in hosting an event at your workplace? Fill out our Google form and someone from NYC PyLadies will reach out.

We look forward to speaking with you!
